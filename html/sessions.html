Sessions

Improved Mental Health for Both Mother and Child

We will be focusing on building positive stories for pregnant teens and gaining back their confidence to face challenges associated with teenage pregnancy, including financial constraints, physical ill-health, difficulties coping at school, social rejection and withdrawal, stigma, and other related psychological constraints. Promoting positive coping mechanisms and stress management techniques to enhance maternal well-being and promote healthy child development through mindfulness practices, gratitude, and exercises.

Meeting Nutritional Requirements for Both Mother and Child

Nutritional education sessions will focus on the importance of a balanced diet during pregnancy and breastfeeding. Monthly food packs will be provided to ensure adequate nutrient intake for both mother and child. Provision of nutritional supplements such as Omega 3 acids and other required health supplements will be provided.

Prevention of STIs, HIV, and AIDS and Ensuring Adherence to Medication for HIV Patients

Comprehensive sexual health education programs aimed at promoting safe sexual practices and preventing sexually transmitted infections (STIs) among pregnant teenagers. Access to HIV testing, counseling, and antiretroviral therapy (ART) for HIV-positive pregnant women, with a focus on medication adherence to prevent mother-to-child transmission of HIV.
