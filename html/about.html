About Us

Feasibility

There is a critical need to address the challenges that pregnant teens face given the fact that they are growing whole human beings who are going to be change-makers in the world. Imagine if all pregnant mothers got support during their pregnancy and had the resources they needed to navigate through these challenges. The world would be filled with healthy growing kids, who are resilient. Through this program, teen moms will not only strive for their health, but they will have the superpower to raise healthy children, free from depression and stress.

Target Population

Super Moms primarily serves pregnant teenagers residing in rural Nkomazi, who are facing challenges and need support. The program intends to serve high school pregnant teenagers including those new to pregnancy and motherhood, as well as those with prior parenting experience.

Size

The starting number is intended to be 10 this year, then the number will increase as we get more resources.

Our Main Focus

Improved Mental Health for Both Mother and Child

Meeting Nutritional Requirements for Both Mother and Child

Prevention of STIs, HIV, and AIDS and Ensuring Adherence to Medication for HIV Patients
